Hyphasia

Field Notes

These notes are written as Hyphasia takes form in the void. It is unclear how much is certain and what remains unshaped. The instrument responds to observation.

Tactile Observations

  • The left mouse button is primary, working all manner of controls.
  • Ctrl-click on a slider permits directly naming a value.
  • A long left click summons a field in the void. The middle mouse button adjusts it.
  • A left double click in the void clears away distraction.

Voices

Particles are energy. There is no sound, no light, without them. Each voice must be built by emitting particles.

Earlier parameters have more power. Lower ones are more subtle.

For a sound to be loud, it must have many particles. A quiet sound will have few. A sudden sound will emit all its particles together. A crescendo grows as a stream.

Phase mod and a particle's acceleration are linked. The results are unpredictable and not always gentle.

Effectors

A very unstable plane. Dynamic and unknown.

Purple fields do nothing, their power latent.

The Attractor's acceleration stimulates phase mod.
