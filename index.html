Hyphasia

Welcome to the Hyphasia website.
This is an experimental sound instrument in active development.

What is this?

Hyphasia is a new kind of synthesizer focused on phase, spectrum, and emergent behavior. It is not finished, not stable, and not fully defined yet.

This site exists to document its development and invite a small group of early users inside while it is still forming.

Current status

Hyphasia is in active development. Builds change frequently, features appear and disappear, and sounds may behave in unexpected ways.

If you are looking for a polished commercial product, this is not it yet.

Get involved

Early access will be available through itch.io. Participants will have direct input into how the instrument evolves.
