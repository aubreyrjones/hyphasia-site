Phase sculpture synthesis

At its deepest level, PSS is based on the Inverse Fast Fourier Transform (IFFT). This well-studied mathematical function converts a spectrum of frequencies, amplitudes, and phases into a perfectly loopable waveform.

IFFT has been studied as a synthesis method since the 70s, yielding a number of techniques such as phase vocoding and spectral synthesis.
